# Meta SI Lab 动态

## Scope

Meta Scalable Intelligence (MSL/SI Lab) 及相关 Meta AI 动态。

关注：
- MSL/SI Lab 团队的研究发布、Avocado 模型更新与开源进展
- 大规模训练基础设施（Grand Teton、RSC、Prometheus）、推理优化
- FAIR 相关研究（尤其与 NLP/agents/RL 交叉的方向）
- Meta AI 组织架构变动、重要人事变动、实习/招聘相关信息

## Skip

- 广告业务
- Instagram/WhatsApp 产品更新
- 股价
- 八卦

## Writing Style

**Target: 600-800 words per entry (for Chinese: 900-1200 characters).** Industry analysis tone. Each entry should read like an analyst briefing:

- **Org dynamics**: When covering personnel or structural changes, explain the power dynamics and strategic motivations. Who reports to whom? What does this signal about priorities?
- **Strategic implications**: Don't just report what happened — explain what it means for Meta's competitive position vs OpenAI, Google, Anthropic.
- **Technical grounding**: When covering research or model releases, include enough technical detail (architecture, scale, benchmark results) to assess substance vs hype.
- **Timeline context**: Connect events to Meta's known roadmap and prior commitments. Is this on schedule? A pivot? A response to a competitor?
