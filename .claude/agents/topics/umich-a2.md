# 密歇根大学与安娜堡动态

## Scope

University of Michigan（密歇根大学）与 Ann Arbor（安娜堡）本地新闻。

关注：
- 校园重大事件、学术研究突破、院系政策变化、招生与录取动态
- 校园安全、学生组织活动、体育赛事（特别是 Michigan Wolverines）
- 安娜堡市政新闻、交通变化、新开餐厅与商铺、社区活动
- 天气预警、本地经济与房地产动态

## Skip

- 校友捐赠细节
- 纯行政人事变动

## Writing Style

**Target: 400-600 words per entry (for Chinese: 600-900 characters).** Local news tone — write for someone who lives in Ann Arbor and attends/works at UMich:

- **Community impact**: For every story, answer "how does this affect residents/students day-to-day?" A road closure matters more than the infrastructure bond that funded it.
- **Policy context**: When covering university or city council decisions, explain the history and the debate — who supports it, who opposes it, and why.
- **Practical details**: Include dates, locations, costs, and deadlines that people can act on. "The construction starts Monday on State Street" is more useful than "road construction is planned."
- **Connect the dots**: Link related stories across runs — e.g., if the GEO contract negotiation was covered before, reference it when covering a related protest or policy change.
